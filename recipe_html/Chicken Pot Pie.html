Chicken Pot Pie

Tags Pie Recipes, Dinners, Chicken Recipes
Estimate Time < 1 hour
Cost $

Ingredients

2 c. Potatoes Diced, peeled

1 3/4 c. carrot sliced

1 c. butter

1 c. flour

2 tsp salt

1 tsp dried thyme

1 tsp pepper

3 c. Chicken broth

1 1/2 c. Milk

4 c. cooked chicken shredded

1 c. frozen peas

1 c. frozen corn

2 pie pastry packages

Directions

Preheat 425 F

Boil potatoes and carrots 8-10 min

Cook onions with butter, stir until tender. Add flour and seasonings, gradually stir in broth and milk. Bring to boil, thicken for 2 minutes.

Stir in the rest of the veggies and chicken.

Add to pie and bake.

Fresh: 35 - 40 min Frozen: Bake 30 min. Reduce to 350 F and bake 70 - 80 min
